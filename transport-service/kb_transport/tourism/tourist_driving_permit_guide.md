# How to Get Your Tourist Driving Permit in Sri Lanka

**Source**: AA Rento (https://aa.rento.lk/en/blog/how-to-get-your-tourist-driving-permit-in-sri-lanka)
**Category**: Tourist Driving Requirements
**Last Updated**: 2025

## Key Requirement: International Driving Permit is MANDATORY

In Sri Lanka, an international driving license alone is NOT sufficient to drive legally. Foreign visitors must obtain a **Tourist Driving Permit** in addition to their International Driving Permit (IDP).

## International Driving Permit (IDP) vs Tourist Driving License

**International Driving Permit (IDP)**:

- Obtained from your home country through organizations like the Automobile Association
- Translates your national driver's license into several languages
- Required document for applying for Sri Lankan tourist permit

**Temporary/Tourist Driving License**:

- Issued by Sri Lankan authorities (DMT or AAC)
- Allows foreign license holders to drive legally in Sri Lanka
- Valid for limited period (30 days to 1 year depending on issuing authority)
- Legal requirement for driving in Sri Lanka

## Required Documents

1. **Passport** with valid tourist visa
2. **Passport-sized photos** (at least two)
3. **International Driving Permit (IDP)** - MANDATORY for processing

## Three Application Options

### Option 1: Department of Motor Traffic (DMT)

- **Location**: Werahera (30 minutes from Colombo)
- **Office Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 4 PM
- **Cost**: LKR 3,500
- **Processing Time**: 3-4 hours
- **Validity**: 30 days
- **Delivery**: Not available; must pick up in person

**Required Documents**:

- Passport
- Copy of passport
- Copy of Sri Lankan visa
- Valid driving license from your country
- Copy of your driving license

**Step-by-Step Process**:

1. Arrive at DMT and head to building "H" (Driving License Unit)
2. Go to Counter 1 for photo and form signing
3. Visit Counters 15-20 for document verification
4. Pay LKR 1,000 fee at designated counter
5. Collect your 30-day temporary driving permit after final check

**Tips**: Arrive early to avoid long queues and have all documents prepared.

### Option 2: Automobile Association of Sri Lanka (AASL)

- **Location**: Colombo 03
- **Office Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 4 PM
- **Cost**: LKR 9,100
- **Processing Time**: 2-3 hours
- **Validity**: 1 year (with IDP)
- **Delivery**: Not available; must pick up in person

**Required Documents**:

- IDP Only
- Copy of passport ID page
- Passport-sized photograph
- Completed application form

**Step-by-Step Process**:

1. Visit the AA Office and go to 3rd floor General Office
2. Collect and complete application form
3. Submit required documents to registrar
4. Make payment at the office
5. Collect temporary driving permit once processing is complete

### Option 3: Rento (Online Application)

- **Location**: Online application via aa.rento.lk
- **Availability**: 24/7 online
- **Cost**: USD 45 (without delivery), USD 46.50 (with delivery)
- **Processing Time**: Typically before arrival or within 1 hour
- **Validity**: 1 year (with IDP)
- **Delivery Options**:
  - Standard delivery anywhere in Sri Lanka (3-4 days)
  - Express delivery within 24 hours (extra charge)
  - Same-day delivery in Colombo and Negombo areas (extra charge)
  - Can deliver to hotel/home/rental before arrival

**Required Documents** (digital versions):

- IDP Only
- Copy of passport ID page
- Digital passport-sized photograph

**Contact Information**:

- Email (Permit): info@rento.lk
- Email (Rentals): rentals@rento.lk
- Address: 31B, Old Kottawa Road, Mirihana, Sri Lanka

## Summary

All three options are legitimate ways to obtain a tourist driving permit in Sri Lanka:

- **DMT**: Most cost-effective (LKR 3,500) but shortest validity (30 days) and longer travel time
- **AASL**: Mid-range price (LKR 9,100), 1-year validity, faster processing, central Colombo location
- **Rento**: Most convenient (online, delivery options), 1-year validity, ideal for travelers on tight schedules

## Important Notes

1. **Both IDP and Tourist Driving Permit are required** - you cannot drive with just an international license
2. Processing times may vary depending on queues and peak seasons
3. Bring extra passport photos and document copies just in case
4. Tourist driving permit allows legal driving for tourists and ensures compliance with local traffic regulations
5. The permit validity period depends on which authority issues it (30 days from DMT, 1 year from AASL/Rento)

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I drive in Sri Lanka with just my Japanese/foreign driving license?**
A: No, you need both an International Driving Permit (IDP) AND a Sri Lankan Tourist Driving Permit.

**Q: Can I drive with just an International Driving Permit?**
A: No, the IDP alone is not sufficient. You must obtain a Tourist Driving Permit from DMT, AASL, or services like Rento.

**Q: Which option is best for tourists?**
A:

- For budget travelers staying less than 30 days: DMT
- For longer stays or those valuing time: AASL or Rento
- For maximum convenience and pre-arrival processing: Rento online service

**Q: How long does each permit last?**
A:

- DMT: 30 days
- AASL: 1 year (with valid IDP)
- Rento: 1 year (with valid IDP)
